About Bharat Pashudhan
Bharat Pashudhan is an initiative by the Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying, Government of India, in collaboration with NDDB. Our mission is to empower livestock owners and field workers with digital tools for animal registration, breed identification, and health management.

We strive to bring transparency, efficiency, and innovation to the livestock sector, ensuring better productivity and welfare for animals and their owners. Our platform leverages modern technology to simplify processes and provide valuable insights for all stakeholders.
